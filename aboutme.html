ABOUT ME & SITE

Hi! My name is Cynthia Wu.

I graduated from my BE in electrical engineering from Stony Brook University in 2022, and am now enrolled for the same major as a master's student at Columbia University.

Originally, my reasoning for entering into the major for my bachelor's was due to a combination of interest and what I felt as the inaccessibility of the subject. I was able to explore a lot of things I probably would have never known to explore as an undergrad! Now, I am enrolled again for a similar reason, to learn the skills necessary to combine health and engineering in the future.

Unfortunately, learning as a student also means forgetting a lot as a student. I would like to collect the tips and tricks I've learned over my few years of learning into a place where I can review it occasionally, and hopefully someone else can benefit from it!

(Naturally due to copyright/legal/moral reasons I will not be posting any class notes nor materials. All items on this site will be things I've thought of outside of class and checked and/or cited to make sure I didn't plagiarize. But it's not like I can just pull things out of my head! Stack Overflow and other sites will definitely be credited many times.)
